QuickBubble is a fast messaging app that requires no account, and allows you to send and receive bubbles instantly⚡

Share a link to a useful resource 🔗
Share a funny picture 😆
Say hello👋
All anonymously 🥸

Privacy is important so bubbles are never recorded, and are only broadcast to chatters who were online at the time of sending 🔒

Write out a message and select the colour or select a background image for your bubble 🎨
Remember, watch yo profanity

Click and drag a bubble to move it across the screen 🫳

Double click a bubble to copy the text 📝
